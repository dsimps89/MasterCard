Monthly Rule

  1. Use the card only for planned purchases you can afford.
  2. About 3–5 days before the 14th, pay the card down to your target reported balance.
  3. Let a small balance report, usually $50–$150.
  4. Pay the full statement balance by the 6th to avoid interest.
  5. Do not spend up to the credit limit. The limit is permission, not a budget.
